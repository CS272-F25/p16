About Smart Grocery

Online shopping website built to help you shop easier and more efficiently

Project Description:

Our project is an interactive online grocery store that is designed to simulate a real e-commerce shopping experience. Our website will allow users to browse items, learn more about each item (like their origin, nutrition facts, and cooking recommendations), add items to cart and submit order forms.

What you will find:

Our project is an interactive online grocery store designed to simulate a real ecommerce shopping experience. You can browse items, find recipies from any country, see the ingredients and instructions, and add them to your cart. All in one place!
