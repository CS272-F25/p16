About Smart Grocery

An online grocery platform, built to help you!

Our Origin Story

Smart Grocery started with a simple question: "Why is online grocery shopping so complicated?"

Founded in 2025 by a group of CS472 students, we set out to build a platform that wasn't just a list of products, but a better list of products

Our Community Philosophy

Sustainability First

We prioritize zero food waste.

Healthy Living

Making nutritional information transparent and accessible for everyone.

Giving Back

The majority of our proceeds go directly out into the community.

The Project

Our project is an interactive online grocery store that is designed to simulate a real e-commerce shopping experience. Our website will allow users to browse items, learn more about each item (like their origin, nutrition facts, and cooking recommendations), add items to cart and submit order forms. We will use our knowledge of HTML, CSS and JS to make a clean and polished site.

View Technical Specs
  • Frontend: HTML5, Bootstrap 5
  • Styling: Custom CSS & Bootstrap Utilities
  • Logic: JavaScript
  • Data: Fetch API & CSV Parsing
